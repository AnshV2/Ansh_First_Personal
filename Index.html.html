ViBot

This bot reads in message with the v!mood command followed by a message. After detecting the mood of the message, the bot then sends a corresponding song that matches the mood and produces an emotion score ranging from -1 (sad) to 1 (happy). Achieved 2nd place overall in YR Hacks 2021 and the best application of a database! Link: https://devpost.com/software/vibot?ref_content=user-portfolio&ref_feature=in_progress
